Patient Policies and Rights

---

### 1. Visitor Policies and Hours
* Daily Visiting Hours: General wards are open to visitors from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. Intensive Care Units (ICUs) have restricted hours from 11:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM.
* Age Restrictions: For infection control and safety, children under the age of 12 are not permitted in inpatient areas unless special authorization is granted by the attending physician.
* Limit: A maximum of two visitors per patient are allowed at any given time.

---

### 2. Patient Rights and Responsibilities
* Rights: Patients have the right to receive considerate and respectful care, obtain complete and current information concerning their diagnosis and treatment, and refuse treatment to the extent permitted by law.
* Responsibilities: Patients are responsible for providing accurate and complete medical histories, following the agreed-upon treatment plan, and treating facility staff and other patients with respect.

---

### 3. Privacy Protocol (HIPAA Statement)
We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of your medical information. In compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), all patient health records, treatment details, and personal data are securely stored and will not be disclosed to unauthorized third parties without explicit, written patient consent, except as required for treatment, payment, or standard healthcare operations.

---

### 4. Feedback and Complaint Handling
We value your feedback to improve our services.
* Submission: Complaints or suggestions can be submitted via the feedback forms available at every nursing station, or emailed to patient-experience@healthcareproject.local.
* Resolution Process: All grievances are reviewed by the Patient Experience Office within 48 hours, and a formal response or resolution plan will be communicated to the patient within 5 business days.

---

### 5. Facility Security Measures
* Identification: All staff must wear visible ID badges. Visitors must register at the reception desk and wear a visitor pass at all times.
* Surveillance: The facility is monitored by 24/7 CCTV in all public corridors, entrances, and waiting areas.
* Inspections: For the safety of everyone, security personnel reserve the right to inspect any bags or packages brought into the facility.
