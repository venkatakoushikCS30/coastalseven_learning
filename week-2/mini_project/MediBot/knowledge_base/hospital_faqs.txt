### HEALTH AND SYMPTOM GUIDANCE (DISCLAIMER: Always seek professional medical advice)
- Headache / Migraine: Head pain can be caused by stress, dehydration, lack of sleep, or sinus issues. Precautions: drink plenty of water, rest in a dark room, avoid screens. If accompanied by sudden numbness, difficulty speaking, or severe neck stiffness, seek emergency care immediately.
- Fever / Body Pain: Typically a sign that the body is fighting an infection (e.g., flu or viral fever). Precautions: rest, stay hydrated, monitor temperature. Seek medical help if fever exceeds 103°F (39.4°C) or lasts more than 3 days.
- Sore Throat / Cough: Often viral (common cold). Precautions: gargle with warm salt water, drink warm fluids, use a humidifier. Consult a doctor if you experience difficulty breathing, swallowing, or if symptoms persist for over a week.
- Indigestion / Stomach Ache: Often due to diet, gas, or mild gastritis. Precautions: eat bland foods, avoid spicy or greasy meals, stay hydrated. If pain is severe, localized in the lower right abdomen (potential appendicitis), or accompanied by vomiting/fever, seek urgent care.
- General Prevention: Eat a balanced diet, exercise at least 30 minutes daily, get 7-8 hours of sleep, wash hands regularly, and stay up to date with vaccinations.
